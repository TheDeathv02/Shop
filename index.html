Types of PCs

There are various types of personal computers available, including desktops, laptops, gaming PCs, and all-in-one PCs. Each type serves different purposes and is suitable for different kinds of users.

  • Desktop PCs: Traditional computers often used for work or home use.
  • Laptops: Portable computers that are convenient for traveling.
  • Gaming PCs: High-performance systems built for gaming.
  • All-in-One PCs: Compact systems with all components integrated into the monitor.
